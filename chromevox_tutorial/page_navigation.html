Moving around a page.

ChromeVox divides a page into groups. In this way, you can get a general overview of the page before diving into something you may be interested in. Try navigating into the following collection of links by pressing ChromeVox down to select the group

  • Home
  • web

Now press ChromeVox up to move back to the collection of links. When you feel comfortable with how this works, move back to this group by pressing ChromeVox down, and then to the next group by pressing ChromeVox down again.

Continue by pressing enter to go to Navigation in Detail.
