Controlling the Voice.

Let's take a break from navigating around the page. ChromeVox comes with the ability to control your speech synthesizer with an assortment of commands that change the rate, pitch, and volume of ChromeVox speech. These commands are listed below. Remember that you can press ChromeVox with right arrow to zoom ChromeVox in to specific objects inside this group of commands.

  • Press ChromeVox hyphen or ChromeVox equals to decrease or increase the speaking rate.
  • Press ChromeVox left bracket or ChromeVox right bracket to decrease or increase the volume of speech.
  • Press ChromeVox semicolon or ChromeVox apostrophe to decrease or increase the pitch of speech.
  • Press the system volume keys (located above the hyphen and equals keys) to change the overall system volume. This will also increase the ChromeVox speech volume.

You can adjust the way your speech sounds at any time.
