HTML5 Audio Description (via text to speech)

This extension plays audio description track elements of HTML5 videos via Chrome text to speech extension.

Text to Speech Settings

 

Keyboard shortcuts

On a webpage:

"Speech" is short for "text to speech"

Ctrl:
Stop speaking
Alt + a:
Use speech default settings
Alt + up arrow:
Increase speech volume
Alt + down arrow:
Decrease speech volume
Alt + c:
Increase speech rate
Alt + z:
Decrease speech rate
Alt + s:
Increase speech pitch
Alt + x:
Decrease speech pitch

When video has focus:

Alt + r:
Detect if audio descriptions on/off
Alt + b:
Turn audio descriptions on/off
Space:
Play/pause video
Left arrow:
Fast forward
Right arrow:
Rewind
Up arrow:
Increase volume of video
Down arrow:
Decrease volume of video
